Emotional Stop Signal Task

Platform Psychopy
History This task is originally developed by: Logan, G. D., & Cowan, W. B. (1984). On the ability to inhibit thought and action: A theory of an act of control. Psychological Review, 91, 295-327.
Publication Yu, C. C., Chen, C. Y., Muggleton, N. G., Ko, C. H., & Liu, S. (2022). Acute exercise improves inhibitory control but not error detection in male violent perpetrators: an ERPs study with the emotional stop signal task. Frontiers in human neuroscience, 16: 796180.

Cyberball Paradigm

Platform Psychopy
History This paradigm is originally developed by Williams, K. D., Cheung, C. K. T., & Choi, W. (2000). CyberOstracism: Effects of being ignored over the Internet. Journal of Personality and Social Psychology, 79, 748-762.

In 2022, this paradigm was modified and published by Schulz, C. C., von Klitzing, K., Deserno, L., Sheridan, M. A., Crowley, M. J., Schoett, M. J., ... & White, L. O. (2022). Emotional maltreatment and neglect impact neural activation upon exclusion in early and mid-adolescence: An event-related fMRI study. Development and psychopathology, 34(2), 573-585.
Description I modified the version of Schulz et al. (2022) and use it for my fNIRS experiment. This tool is publicly available at [link will be posted soon]. If you use this tool, I would appreciate citations to the papers listed above.
Updated Jul-14-23
